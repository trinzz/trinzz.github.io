Checklist
  • Dataset provenance documented (sources, constraints, ownership)
  • Versioned release register with sign-off criteria
  • Expert review policy, consensus rules, and QC coverage
  • Reliability metrics with thresholds (agreement, stability, subgroup)
  • Monitoring baselines (PSI) + documented remediation playbooks
