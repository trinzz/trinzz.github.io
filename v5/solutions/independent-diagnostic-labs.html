Why “good enough” AI is a liability in diagnostics

In diagnostics, small errors are not noise — they become clinical risk and reputational damage. Reliability must be measurable before adoption.

  • Lack of traceability makes incidents hard to investigate
  • Inconsistent labeling creates unpredictable outcomes
  • Drift over time can silently degrade performance
  • No sign-off criteria means adoption is guesswork
