WSI variability is the reliability bottleneck

Even strong models fail when stain/scanner/site shifts are not measured. Reliability must be proven at the dataset layer, not assumed at the model layer.

  • Stain and scanner differences shift distributions
  • Region-level labels need consensus and reconciliation
  • Rare morphologies are underrepresented and high-risk
  • Dataset updates without drift baselines break trust
