The hidden problem: site variability becomes institutional risk

When site A performs well and site B fails, the organization loses trust in the entire deployment. Reliability must be measured and governed at the network level.

  • Protocol differences change distributions across sites
  • Scanner diversity creates silent performance gaps
  • Patient mix shifts cohort characteristics by region
  • No unified release discipline across departments/vendors
