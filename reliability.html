FAQ
Common questions from clinical and enterprise buyers.
What is a Reliability Audit, exactly?

A structured assessment of traceability, QC strength, consensus integrity, shift readiness, and subgroup risk—delivered as an executive report plus an implementation roadmap.

How is CG‑DBS different from an audit?

CG‑DBS produces a benchmark score and pillar breakdown for quick alignment and prioritization; an audit is deeper and includes remediation design.

Do you support ongoing monitoring programs?

Yes. We define thresholds (PSI, calibration) and a clinical review workflow so interventions are measurable and auditable.
