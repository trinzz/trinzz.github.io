Your real requirement: defensible deployment decisions

In hospitals, trust is institutional. The question is not “does it work?” — it’s “can we prove why we trust it, and how we keep it safe over time?”

  • Documented validation methodology and sampling strategy
  • Traceability and accountability (who approved what)
  • Release criteria aligned to intended use
  • Monitoring plan and re-certification triggers
