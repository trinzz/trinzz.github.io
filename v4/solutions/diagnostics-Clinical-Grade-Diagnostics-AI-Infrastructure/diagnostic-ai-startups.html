Why your best pilot still fails in production

Production introduces new scanners, protocols, sites, and edge-case prevalence. Without measured dataset reliability and governed releases, small distribution changes become clinical trust failures.

  • Label inconsistency inflates pilot performance and collapses later
  • Edge cases are underrepresented and untracked
  • Dataset versions change without a release register
  • Drift remains invisible until clinicians lose trust

What hospitals actually want from you

Hospitals don’t ask for better slogans. They ask for proof: how you validated, what thresholds you met, what you will monitor, and how you will respond when the world changes.

  • Reliability scorecard with thresholds (agreement, stability, subgroup robustness)
  • Traceability map (source → label → reviewer → version → release)
  • Certification report for governance review
  • Monitoring baselines (PSI) + remediation playbooks
