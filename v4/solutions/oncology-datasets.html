Why oncology datasets are the hardest to defend

Governance teams want proof that high-risk subgroups are covered and that dataset updates won’t silently break performance.

  • Rare-case scarcity (sampling bias)
  • Label ambiguity (grading/staging edge cases)
  • Protocol heterogeneity across sites
  • Distribution drift mistaken as ‘model degradation’
