Why enterprise AI deals stall

Procurement and clinical governance teams do not approve “accuracy claims.” They approve evidence packages: how the dataset was validated, how it is versioned, what thresholds it meets, and how drift is monitored in production.

  • Dataset validity is unclear (sampling, bias, and label consistency)
  • Traceability is incomplete (who labeled what; which version produced results)
  • Release discipline is missing (no formal sign-off criteria)
  • Monitoring is absent (drift appears as silent failure)

How engagements start

Most teams begin with a short reliability audit sprint. We establish baselines, identify gaps, and define the fastest path to certify the next dataset release.

  • Week 1: scope + sampling strategy + baseline reliability scoring
  • Week 2: traceability gaps + remediation plan + release thresholds
  • Optional: certify the next release and set monitoring baselines (PSI)
