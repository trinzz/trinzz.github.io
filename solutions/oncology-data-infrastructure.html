The Oncology AI Data Challenge

Oncology AI systems depend on integrating imaging, pathology, genomic, and longitudinal clinical data. Most cancer datasets lack structured alignment, traceable annotation history, and measurable reliability validation.

These inconsistencies introduce noise, bias, and instability that limit model performance and delay clinical deployment.

Cancer AI teams need infrastructure designed specifically for multimodal consistency and reliability.
